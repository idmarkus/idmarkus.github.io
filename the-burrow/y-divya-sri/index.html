3 ways to not stress

My brain is in pain. I successfully reduced the pain by doing the following.

This process was created at the end of utopian thinking. Inspired by ‘pataphysics – the science of imaginary solutions, with the motif of a self-similar spiral, Bernoulli described as “I arise the same, though changed,” these practises form a coda of repeated memory. In today’s popular self-help books our experiences are flattened; the simplified is easier to book. Opening such exercises to the absurd, we can engage our humor to find a solution to our problem extending to the limit of the imagined.

    QUESTIONS
  1. Is being attached to living beings, other than humans, better for one?
  2. How can i escape visceral paranoia?
  3. To what extent can i analyse a problem before blame becomes the problem?
  4. Can the process of mentally beating oneself up cause blindness?
  5. What are the maximum number of associations one can draw to a thing before turning delusional?
  6. Can i couple vulnerability with courage?
  7. Is self-induced pain turning into a fashion?

Writing

PRACTICE 2_written all over

Make your own list of stress triggers.
Construct sentences by combining triggers with objects from P1.
Pick a sentence to write repeatedly and fill a page.
