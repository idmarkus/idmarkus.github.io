A script selecting short fragments of video from the artist's personal archive, making polyptychs of up to 36 frames of time. Due to the random nature of the selection most results exhibit nothing, as incoherent memories. With this, the few sequences that happen to give glimpses of recognition become precious. A somber beauty of time and chance.
